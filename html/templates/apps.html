Galaxy Harvester Mobile Apps

The Galaxy Harvester Mobile App provides features to let you do research while you're out and about, or assist you while in game without switching out to a browser on your PC.

Features:

  • Login/Logout/Register/Password Reset
  • Select Galaxy
  • Add/Edit Resources
  • Add/Edit Waypoints
  • Browse Recent Activity
  • Search Resources By Type, Group, Quality, Availability, Name, Favorites
  • Manage Spawn and Despawn Alert Filters
  • Get Alerts inside Mobile App
  • Browse News
  • OCR Partial Resource Add assist with camera
